template<typename realT, class inputSpectT>
class mx::AO::aoSystem< realT, inputSpectT >

Describes an analytic adaptive optics (AO) system.

Templatized by the turbulence power spectral density (PSD) and the wavefront sensor (WFS) sensitivity function.

Worker function for raw contrast fuctions.

Most of the logic in this calculation is the same, except for the calculation of variance. The varFunc function pointer is used to calculate the variance. This handles the other details such as Strehl normalization, fitting error (if appropriate), and bounds checking.
