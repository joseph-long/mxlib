template<typename _realT>
struct mx::wfp::idealCoronagraph< _realT >

The Ideal Coronagraph.

A simple toy coronagraph which operates only the pupil plane, subtracting the energy-minimizing wavefront [2]. For an on-axis source with a perfectly flat wavefront this results in perfect extinction. This coronagraph is not physically realizable, but it is often useful for modeling and analysis, and it has been shown that real-world optimized coronagraphs often approach ideal performance [19] [and Males in prep].
